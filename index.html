Frequently Asked Questions

8 most common questions · Answers to every query · For more questions, call or WhatsApp us

+
How long does an RO purifier last?
We use premium parts — easily 8-10 years. Filters need replacement every 12 months. 3-year warranty + 5-year extended option available.
+
What are the service charges? Any hidden costs?
No hidden costs. First-time installation is FREE. AMC plans ₹999-5,999/year (4 tiers). On SLA breach, service-free + parts-free guarantee.
+
Is EMI / Cash on Delivery available?
Yes — EMI options available (3-12 months · zero interest). We also accept Cash on Delivery. UPI/Card/Netbanking all accepted.
+
Does Made-to-Order mean delayed delivery?
Just 2-3 working days. Each machine is specially assembled to your needs — using stock parts. Premium quality with zero compromise.
+
When do filters need replacement? Will I get reminders?
Auto reminders in the app (10-12 months depending on usage). Smart TDS sensor tracks water quality in real-time. You won't have to remember.
+
What is the refund policy? How soon can I return?
7-day no-questions-asked refund. Full refund even after installation if unsatisfied. Owner calls directly on your issues — Apple-style commitment.
+
Which model is best for hard water?
GF-Pro (Best Seller) recommended for high TDS. Get a free TDS test — we will give you the exact recommendation. Special calibration for Rajasthan.
+
What does the warranty cover?
Pump · Membrane · Electronics · SMPS · Tank — all 3 years. Filters are consumables (1 year). Extended 5-year warranty option for ₹2,499.
